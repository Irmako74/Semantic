NASA Mars Orbiter Releasing One of Its Last Rainbow-Colored Maps

Six Views of Nili Fossae, as Seen by MRO’s CRISM: Seen are six views of the Nili Fossae region of Mars captured by the Compact Reconnaissance Imaging Spectrometer for Mars, or CRISM, one of the instruments aboard NASA’s Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.
